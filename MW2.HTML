HeatIQ - Smart cooking solutions

This project aims to design a smart stove system and companion mobile app that simplify cooking and enhance safety for multitasking users. By integrating features like real-time monitoring, heat automation, and intuitive controls, it provides a seamless and efficient cooking experience.

O'er lanes - Game design

O'er Lanes is a mobile game with a simple but addictive concept. It's an endless arcade hopper where you control a character and try to navigate busy roads, train tracks, and rivers. You hop from platform to platform, dodging traffic and other hazards to get as far as possible.

NexGate - The smart way in

NexGate is an innovative smart door solution that enhances home security and convenience. It offers seamless communication and real-time updates between residents and visitors, ensuring a safe and efficient entry process. With a focus on user-friendly interfaces and reliable access control, NexGate redefines home interactions.

Graphic Zine - A day in a design school

The "A Day in Design School" zine is like a colorful trip through creativity using only two colors. It shows the fun and learning in design school with simple pictures and words. Even with just two colors, it captures the excitement of learning and creating. It shows that even with limits, creativity can still shine bright.
